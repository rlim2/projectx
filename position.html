Predoctoral and Postdoctoral Positions

Predoctoral research assistant and postdoc positions are available at Dr. Xia's Lab. Applicants who majored in Biomedical Engineering, Applied Physics, Optics, Mechanical Engineering, or a related engineering field are encouraged to apply. Preferences will be given to candidates with background in optics, ultrasonics or imaging.

Interested students may email curriculum vitae to Dr. Xia at junxia@buffalo.edu.
